About Us

Our History

The Social Science Department at AASTU has a long-standing tradition of excellence in education and research, dedicated to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and global citizenship among its students.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing high-quality education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical perspectives needed to understand complex social issues and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution recognized nationally and globally for our innovative teaching methods, impactful research, and contributions to social science education and practice.

Join Us on Our Journey

At the Social Science Department, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and societies. We invite prospective students, researchers, and partners to join us on our journey of discovery, learning, and growth.
